Equipment

Enter details of any magic items you hold.

When you have completed this section move on to the select your output options and submit the return. There are three output options:
  • Narrative export: The default, downloads a text file you can e-mail to your LO
  • CSV export: Only available if your LO agrees. Downloads a CSV file ready for copy / paste into the returns spreadsheet that you can e-mail to your LO
  • Google Docs export: Only available if your LO agrees. Uploads your data to a central branch Google Docs spreadsheet for your LO to access
The only other option is to Remember me on this computer. This will place a number of Cookies on your PC with basic data about your return allowing key fields to be automatically pre-populated for next months return.
