Income and Expenses
There are three fields to populate here. Each field accepts a value in crowns and a value in pennies. The three fields are:
  • Balance from last month
  • Any income this month other than that derived from positions or guild membership (currently Guards, Foresters, Magistrates or Judges)
  • Any expenses other than Guild fees or Church / Order tithes

Once you have completed this tab move on to the Fees and Tithes tab.
